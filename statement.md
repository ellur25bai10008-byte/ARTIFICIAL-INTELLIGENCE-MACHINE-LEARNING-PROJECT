#PROBLEM STATEMENT 
Hotel Booking Management System
Managing hotel reservations through traditional methods such as phone calls, walk-ins, or manual registers often leads to inefficiencies and customer dissatisfaction. Common issues include:
- Double-bookings and errors due to lack of real-time room availability tracking.
- Delayed confirmations that frustrate customers and reduce trust in the service.
- Limited transparency about hotel facilities, pricing, and amenities.
- Poor integration with hotel operations such as employee management and customer feedback.
- Restricted accessibility, as customers must physically visit or call hotels to make reservations.
These challenges highlight the need for a centralized, automated hotel booking management system that provides:
- Real-time room availability and instant booking confirmation.
- EASY ACCESS FOR  customers.
- Detailed hotel information including facilities, pricing.
- Integrated modules for customer feedback.
- A scalable, secure platform capable of handling peak booking seasons.
